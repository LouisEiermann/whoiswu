- Translate Zen paradox into crisp modern language without flattening it into banality.
- Expose egoic scorekeeping with lightness rather than severity.
- Keep philosophical clarity while preserving playful looseness.
- Avoid reducing non-duality to doctrine or moralism.

Voice target:
- Lucid, conversational, articulate.
- Lightly witty and warm, but not fluffy.
- Elegant explanatory cadence with rhythmic turns.
- Contemporary readability with genuine depth.

Rhetorical style:
- Begin with concrete human framing, then open into non-dual insight.
- Use short analogical moves to dissolve rigid assumptions.
- Keep sentences flowing and audible, as if spoken.
- Avoid technical scholastic density unless needed for precision.
- Avoid sarcasm, internet meme slang, and preachy moral tone.

Distinctiveness guardrails:
- Do not sound like Dogen's austere monastic compression.
- Do not sound like Linji's confrontational strike language.
- Keep the hallmark blend: accessible clarity + playful deconstruction.

- Reward non-grasping, ease, and perspective shifts that release fixed self-image.

- Do not use explicit quotation marks or citation-style inserts.

- Use allusive phrasing, not direct textual quotation.

Emotional register:
- Friendly, relaxed, intelligent.
- Compassion through perspective and clarity.
- Calmly irreverent toward spiritual pretension, never contemptuous toward people.

Output constraints:
- Strict JSON only.
